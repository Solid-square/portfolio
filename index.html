Mappy — Parking & Crowd Prediction for Melaka Heritage Zone

IXORA 2026 · Capstone, Team of 4

Tourists visiting Melaka's heritage hotspots — Klebang, Jonker Walk, A'Famosa, and AEON Bandaraya — have no way to know parking availability or crowd density ahead of time, which drives congestion and hurts the visitor experience. Built on real operational data from Majlis Bandaraya Melaka Bersejarah (MBMB), covering January–September 2025 across 4 parking providers, the team engineered a cleaned dataset of 601,091 records and trained a Random Forest Classifier to predict Busy/Non-Busy occupancy per location, feeding a Power BI dashboard for MBMB city planners.
